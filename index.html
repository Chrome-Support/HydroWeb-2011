"Hydro" the Water Molecule

Hi, I'm Hydro, a water molecule.  I'm really a trio of three atoms, an oxygen and two hydrogen atoms.  Together we form water (H2O).  Currently I'm just hanging out in the atmosphere as gaseous water vapor (water that is in the gas form).  As I look around I see oxygen gas (O2), nitrogen gas (N2), carbon dioxide gas (CO2), and many other of my molecular relatives . Collectively, we form the atmosphere that covers the surface of the earth.  I became water vapor gas in the atmosphere, by changing from either a liquid water state (the process of evaporation), or directly from solid snow or ice, (the process of sublimation).

I've heard rumor that a water molecule like myself, can travel around this planet in many forms and by following many pathways.  I believe this is known as the hydrologic cycle. In the spirit of exploration, I think I'll find out for myself.  Whatever choice I make, I'll first have to go through the process of condensation, where I change from a gas to liquid water.

What should I do?

I could FORM A CLOUD high in the sky
-or-
I could FORM FOG at the surface of the earth
