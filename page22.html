Here I am on the surface of a leaf.  What a beautiful day.  I think I'll transpire into the atmosphere.  Transpiration refers to water that escapes from the leaves of a plant and evaporates into the atmosphere.  Often, evaporation and transpiration are combined into one term, evapotranspiration.

There is only one thing I can do, and that is ENTER THE ATMOSPHERE THROUGH THE PROCESS OF TRANSPIRATION ( a form of evaporation)
