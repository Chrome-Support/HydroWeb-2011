I'm on the surface of the ground.  It looks like a rich loamy soil.

What should I do?

I could soak into the soil and INFILTRATE (the process of infiltration)
-or-
I could join other water molecules and COLLECT IN A PUDDLE  on the ground (a process known as depression storage)
-or-
I could RUNOFF THE SURFACE OF THE LAND (this is known as overland flow)
-or-

I could EVAPORATE
