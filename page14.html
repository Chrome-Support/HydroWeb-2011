These plant cells could either subject me to the process of photosynthesis or allow me out onto the surface of the leaves.  If I go through photosynthesis, I will be combined with carbon dioxide, using the energy of sunlight, to make  me into a sugar molecule (C6H12O6).  This would make me a type of carbohydrate.  Starches are another type.  This is outrageous!  What about my identity?

What should I do?

I could go through photosynthesis and BE MADE INTO A CARBOHYDRATE AND STORED IN ROOT
-or-
I could go through photosynthesis and BE MADE INTO A CARBOHYDRATE AND EATEN BY A PERSON
-or-
I could remain a water molecule and  MOVE ONTO THE SURFACE OF A LEAF THROUGH TINY PORES (thus going through the process of transpiration)
