I'm sitting as a puddle of water on the surface of the ground.  This is a form of what is known as depression storage. I now have to choose from one of three choices.  I can change from liquid water  to water vapor (evaporation), soak into the ground (infiltration), or flow along the surface of the ground (overland flow).

What should I do?

I could INFILTRATE INTO THE GROUND
-or-
I could follow the downhill slope and RUNOFF THE LAND AS OVERLAND FLOW
-or-
I could EVAPORATE INTO THE ATMOSPHERE
