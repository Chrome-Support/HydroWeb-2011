Misting around as fog (small droplets of liquid water) near the surface of the earth is rather enjoyable.  Now that I part of a very small droplet of liquid water floating around this forest, I'll have to make a decision.

What should I do?

I could EVAPORATE (meaning that I will go through the process of evaporation and change from liquid water back into water vapor)
-or-
I could ATTACH TO THE SURFACE OF A PLANT (this is known as the process of interception)
