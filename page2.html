Brrr, I'm Snow!  Snow is just a special form of solid water, more commonly known as ice.
If only I can warm up just a little bit. 

What should I do?

I could SUBLIMATE.  Sublimation is occurs when a solid changes directly to a gas, without melting into a liquid first.
-or-
I could MELT. Melting occurs when a solid changes into a liquid.
